About PadVending

PadVending is a social enterprise dedicated to improving menstrual hygiene access through innovative technology. We design and deploy smart sanitary pad vending machines in educational institutions, public spaces, and workplaces across India.

Our Mission

To ensure every person has access to sanitary products when they need them, eliminating the stigma and inconvenience associated with menstrual emergencies.

Our Technology

  • IoT-enabled real-time stock monitoring
  • Secure QR-based payments via Razorpay
  • Automatic dispensing with IR verification
  • Low-stock alerts for maintenance teams
