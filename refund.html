Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: June 18, 2026

Important: Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method (UPI/bank account).

1. Eligibility for Refund

You are eligible for a refund if:

  • Payment was successful but product was not dispensed
  • Partial dispensing occurred (e.g., paid for 2, got 1)
  • Machine malfunction prevented dispensing

2. Refund Amount

Full refund: If no product was dispensed.
Partial refund: If partial quantity was dispensed (refund = amount for undispensed items).

3. Refund Process

Refunds are automatically initiated by our system when IR sensors detect dispensing failure. For manual requests, contact us within 24 hours with your transaction ID.

4. Cancellation

Orders cannot be cancelled after payment is processed. Refund policy applies instead.
